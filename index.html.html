Karate History

Karate has a rich and fascinating history that dates back centuries. Here's a brief overview:

Karate originated in Okinawa, Japan, as a form of self-defense developed by the Ryukyu Kingdom. It was influenced by Chinese martial arts and indigenous Okinawan fighting styles.

During the early 20th century, karate began to spread beyond Okinawa to mainland Japan and eventually internationally, thanks to practitioners like Gichin Funakoshi, who is often credited with popularizing karate on the Japanese mainland.

Today, karate is practiced worldwide and is known for its emphasis on discipline, respect, and self-improvement. It has evolved into various styles and schools, each with its unique techniques and philosophies.

At DoubleJ Dojo, we honor the traditions and teachings of karate while also embracing modern training methods to help our students become skilled martial artists and well-rounded individuals.

FAQ

Got questions? We have answers!

What should I wear to class?

We recommend wearing comfortable clothing that allows for easy movement, such as a t-shirt and sweatpants. Additionally, please remove any jewelry and tie back long hair.

Do I need any prior experience to join?

No prior experience is necessary! Our classes are suitable for beginners and experienced practitioners alike. Our instructors will guide you through the training process at your own pace.
